Benefits of cloud computing :

cost-effective : 
you pay for what you consume, no up-fornt cost . pay as you go thousands of customers sharing 
the cost of the resources

global : launch workloads anywhere in the world, just choose a region

secure : cloud provider takes care of physical security. cloud services can buy secure 
by default or you have the ability to configure access down to granular level

reliable : data backup, disaster recovery, and data replication, and fault tolerance

scalable : increase or decrease resources and services based on demand 

elastic : automate scaling during spikes and drop in demand 

current : the underlying hardware and managed software is patched , upgraded and replaced
by the cloud provider without interruption to you
